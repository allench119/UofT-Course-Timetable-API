The following timetable information is the most current information available. All changes (additional courses, meeting sections, time changes, course or meeting cancellations) since the original on-line date will be noted in the Information Updated column.

For LMP365H1, LMP406H1 and LMP415H1 'PE' indicator courses: Non-Pathobiology Specialists may be considered for enrolment. If space allows, students with similar backgrounds may be given special permission to enroll. For further information and/or instructions, contact the LMP Undergraduate Office, Medical Sciences Bldg (MSB), room 6209. (Earliest consideration for enrolment starts in June until August).

For LMP405Y1 (Project in Laboratory Medicine & Pathobiology): contact the LMP Undergraduate Office, Medical Science Bldg, room 6209 or visit the departmental website: http://www.lmp.utoronto.ca/course/lmp405y1

For 'E' indicator courses: contact the LMP Undergraduate Office prior to the end of August, Medical Science Bldg (MSB), room 6209.
